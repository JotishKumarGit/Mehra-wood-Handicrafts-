About Us

MEHRA WOOD HANDICRAFTS – Trusted for Premium Wooden Serveware & Handcrafted Products

Established with a passion for craftsmanship, Mehra Wood Handicrafts is a trusted name in the world of premium wooden products. We specialize in creating high-quality wooden trays, platters, bowls, jewellery boxes, and cutlery that combine elegance, durability, and traditional artistry. Every piece is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans, blending natural beauty with practical functionality. Our mission is to deliver sustainable, stylish, and timeless wooden products that add charm to homes, hotels, restaurants, and gifting collections. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Mehra Wood Handicrafts continues to be a preferred choice for both domestic and international clients.
